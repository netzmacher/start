Spell Check
===========

Start supports spell checking while writing. Unknown or wrong words will get
a red underline. You can manage the substitution with

* [CTRL] + right button of your mouse


Preconditions
-------------

* Your browser supports spell checking.

* There must included a page TypoScript. See: :ref:`Administrators > Best Practice > Spell Check <administrators_bestpractice_rte_searchspellcheckyoutube>`.
